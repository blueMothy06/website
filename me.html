Artist Statement


My artwork serves to immerse the audience in a moment. Life is always moving from one point to another, so fast that you don't even realize it sometimes. My work aims to slow down a point in time and take a closer look at the things that are going on. While not all of my pieces will be based on real life, if the atmopshere of a piece transcends the drawing's limitations, I can be satisfied.
