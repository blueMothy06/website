Unconventional Art

Finished: February 2021
Online communication is how most of us talk to other people these days. It is lacking in a physical form and a lot of the experience is meeting people is lost through a screen. However, despite the limitations that the lockdown has placed on us, I managed to make some close friends that I don't think I would've made, if the pandemic had not happened. This picture depicts a flower growing out a phone screen, under an artifical light, which reflects how even geniune friendship can blossom in this environment.
